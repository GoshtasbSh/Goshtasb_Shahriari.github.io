My name is Goshtasb Shahriari Mehr. I am a PhD student in Design, Construction, and Planning at the University of Florida. Additionally, I am a Master's student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Florida. I work as a Graduate Research Assistant at the Florida Institute for Built Environment Resilience (FIBER). Previously, I earned my Master's degree in Geographic Information Science from the University of Tehran and my Bachelor's degree in Civil-Surveying Engineering from Khaje Nasir Toosi University of Technology, Tehran.

Research Interests

My main research interests include spatio-temporal analysis, transportation planning, recommender systems, and spatial analysis.
